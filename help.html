The menu across the top shows you all that you can manage in the RUCAM system. Below shows the basics of creating, editing and searching records. Some items in this menu have more complex functionality which is described under the tabs above.

The system also allows you to allow cards to enter venues on a certain date for a fixture.

Viewing Records

By clicking on an item in the menu you will be able to view all the records for that item. You will then see another menu, such as the one below, giving you the options to create a new record or search for a particular record.

Creating Records

By clicking on the create new button you will be shown a form, below on the right, where you will be able to enter the data to create a new record. To save the data you must click the create new button located below the form.

Editing Records

To edit a record click the ID number for that record as shown below.

All records have an update page like the one shown below. From here you can edit the record and save the changes by clicking the update button located below the form.

Some items, such as competitors, teams and fixtures, have more functions to do with the record shown below this. For more imformation on this click on the different tabs at the top of this help page.

Searching Records

By clicking on the search button you will shown a form to enter your search criteria. Clicking search will show the results in the table to the left.

There are two search conditions AND and OR. AND means that all of the search fields you enter must exist in the record for it to be shown in the results. OR means that any of the search fields can exist in the record for it to be shown in the results.

In the update section of a team, underneath the update form are two more forms shown here on the right. The first form is for issuing and expiring all the cards belonging to this team. The second is for giving all the cards in this team authorsation to the fixtures they are participating in.

Issuing Cards

To issue cards to all the competitors in this team the Team Member Cards form can be used. You must enter when the cards will be valid from and when they will expire, and I needn't tell you the expiry date must be after the valid from date. Clicking issue cards will then issue a card to each of the competitors belonging to this team.

Expiring Cards

To expire all the cards belonging to this teams competitors can be done with the red Expire All Cards button. It is red for a reason, once done none of the teams cards will be able to enter venues they had authorisation for.

Authorisation To Venues

The second form is for giving all the cards belonging to this team authorisation to the selected fixtures. To do this tick the boxes for the fixtures you want this team to have access to and click Add Authorisation to apply the authorisation to all the teams cards.

If you notice under the fixture name it shows you how many of the teams' cards are authorised for that fixture, also the fixtures shown in bold indicate the fixtures this team is actually playing in.

In the update section of a competitor, underneath the update form is another form, Competitor's Card, shown here on the right. You will notice in the image it says that this competitor does not have a card, it is there where it will show you whether the competitor has a card or not and whether it is valid or expired.

Issue Card

Here is another place where you can issue a card to a competitor, to do this enter the valid from and valid until dates and click Issue Card.

Replace Card

So competitors are human and there will always be that one that left their card on the bus, well there is a button for this. By entering the valid from and valid until dates and clicking Replace Card the competitors old card will be cancelled, so that person who found the card on the bus cannot use it to get into the venue, and a new one will be issued copying all the authorisations from the lost one to the new one.

Expire Card

Here is another place where you can expire a compettitor's card by clicking the red Expire Card button. This will prevent this card from being used to access a venue.

Authorising Fixtures

When creating or updating a competitor there is another table listing the fixtures, shown on the right, this is for selecting or editing the fixtures that this card has authorisation for.

To authorise the card for a fixture, tick the box corresponding to that fixture, to remove authorisation untick that box.
